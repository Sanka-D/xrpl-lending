FAQ

Atlas is a permissionless lending protocol built natively on the XRP Ledger. Anyone can create isolated lending markets with custom collateral, oracles, and risk parameters. No governance, no fees, no gatekeepers.

Smart contracts are arriving on the XRP Ledger, opening the door to a new generation of DeFi primitives. Atlas is positioned to be one of the first permissionless lending protocols on XRPL, leveraging native settlement, low fees, and direct access to XRP and RLUSD liquidity.

Atlas will support every major asset on XRPL: XRP, RLUSD, ETH, BTC, USDC, ONDO and more. Because markets are permissionless, anyone can deploy a market for any asset pair with the oracle and risk model of their choice.

Atlas is in active development alongside the XRPL smart contract rollout. We're targeting a launch as soon as the core infrastructure is production-ready. Use the contact form to stay in the loop or get involved.

Most lending protocols on EVM chains are governed pools with curated assets. Atlas takes a different approach: isolated, immutable, permissionless markets on a chain that has never had on-chain lending. Combined with XRPL's low fees and fast finality, this unlocks designs that simply weren't possible before.
